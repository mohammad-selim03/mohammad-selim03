### 📊 SaaS Analytics Dashboard — B2B Platform
**The Problem:** A B2B startup had raw data but no way to present it to clients. Their team was manually generating reports in spreadsheets — wasting 20+ hours/week.

**What I Built:** A full-stack SaaS dashboard with role-based access, real-time charts, automated PDF reporting, and a white-label option for their enterprise clients.

**Stack:** `Next.js` · `TypeScript` · `PostgreSQL` · `Prisma` · `Tailwind CSS` · `Chart.js` · `Stripe`

**Result:**
- ⬇️ Reporting time reduced from 20 hrs/week → 0 (fully automated)
- 📈 Client retention improved — dashboard became a key selling point
- 💰 Unlocked a new Enterprise tier worth $3k+/month per client

---

### 🛒 Headless E-Commerce Platform — DTC Brand
**The Problem:** A growing brand was stuck on a slow, template-based Shopify store that couldn't handle custom logic or their aggressive growth targets.

**What I Built:** A headless Next.js storefront with custom cart logic, dynamic pricing rules, loyalty integration, and sub-1s page loads.

**Stack:** `Next.js` · `TypeScript` · `Tailwind CSS` · `Shopify API` · `Node.js` · `MongoDB`

**Result:**
- ⚡ Page load time dropped from 4.8s → 0.9s
- 📈 Conversion rate improved by ~22% post-launch
- 🏆 Handled a flash sale with 8,000 concurrent users — zero downtime

---

### 🤖 AI-Powered Document Intelligence Tool
**The Problem:** A legal-tech startup needed a way for their clients to upload contracts and instantly extract key clauses, risks, and summaries — without paying $500/hr lawyer fees.

**What I Built:** A full-stack AI web app with document upload, GPT-4 powered extraction, structured output rendering, and a saved history dashboard.

**Stack:** `Next.js` · `TypeScript` · `OpenAI API` · `Node.js` · `PostgreSQL` · `AWS S3`

**Result:**
- 🧠 Processes a 40-page contract in under 30 seconds
- 💼 Used by 200+ SMB clients in the first 3 months post-launch
